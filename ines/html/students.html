Hi, I’m Ines.

I am a passionate piano teacher with over 20 years of experience, committed to inspiring and empowering students to reach their full potential.

我是一位充满激情的钢琴老师，拥有超过20年的教学经验，致力于激励和赋能学生实现他们的潜力。

As a senior registered piano teacher with the British Columbia Music Teachers Association (BCRMTA) and the Royal Conservatory of Music (RCM) in Canada, I bring professionalism and expertise to every lesson. Additionally, I am an instructor for the Canadian Young Artists Model Award.

我是加拿大BC省音乐教师协会注册钢琴老师（BCRMTA），加拿大RCM皇家音乐学院注册钢琴老师，加拿大青少年艺术家模范奖导师。

I hold a bachelor’s degree in piano and have served as an associate professor at a school of arts, as well as a judge for piano grade examinations. My students have achieved outstanding success, earning awards in prestigious competitions such as Kiwanis, Little Mozart, Crescendo, Elite International Competition, and the Canada Festival.

毕业于专业音乐院校钢琴系。艺术学院副教授，钢琴考级评委。多名学生在kiwanis、little Mozart 、Crescendo 、Elite international competition 、Canada festival 等重要钢琴🎹比赛中获奖🥇🥇🥇

Known for my professional, rigorous, and engaging teaching style, I am highly regarded by both children and parents. I specialize in tailoring lessons to each student’s unique abilities and needs, creating personalized and scientific teaching plans that enable rapid and significant progress in a short time.

二十几年钢琴教学经验，通过专业、严谨、有趣的教学方法，深受孩子们和家长的喜爱。 善于因材施教，根据学生的不同特点和需求，科学制定教学方案，让学生的演奏水平在短期内得到快速提升。

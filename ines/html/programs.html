At Ines Piano Academy, we offer a comprehensive Royal Conservatory of Music (RCM) course designed to prepare students for success in their RCM exams. Our program is crafted to meet the rigorous standards of the RCM while providing a supportive and personalized learning environment.

Why choose Ines Piano Academy for RCM preparation?

Extensive Experience
With years of experience in preparing students for RCM exams, Ines has developed effective teaching strategies that cater to the unique needs of each student. Her expertise ensures that students are well-prepared and confident on exam day.
Personalized Lesson Plans
We understand that every student learns at their own pace. Our lesson plans are tailored to meet individual goals, ensuring that each student receives the attention and guidance they need to succeed.
Supportive Learning Environment
At Ines Piano Academy, we foster a nurturing and encouraging atmosphere. Our goal is to build students' confidence and help them develop the skills necessary to excel in their musical journey.
Proven Track Record
Our students consistently achieve high marks in their RCM exams. This success is a testament to the quality of instruction and the dedication of our teaching staff.

Our RCM course covers all levels, from beginner to advanced, and includes:

Comprehensive Music Theory
Understanding the theoretical aspects of music is crucial for RCM exams. Our lessons cover all necessary theory components in depth.
Technical Skills
We emphasize the development of strong technical skills, including scales, arpeggios, and finger exercises.
Repertoire
Students will learn and master pieces from the RCM syllabus, ensuring they are well-prepared for their exams.
Ear Training
These essential skills are integrated into our lessons to provide a well-rounded musical education.

Beginner Piano Lessons

Our beginner piano lessons are perfect for those who are just starting their musical journey. We focus on building a solid foundation while making learning enjoyable and engaging.

• Fundamental Skills: Students will learn the basics of piano playing, including proper hand positioning, posture, and technique.
• Basic Music Theory: Understanding the fundamentals of music theory is essential for young musicians. We introduce them in a fun and accessible way.
• Note Reading: Learning to read music is a key component of our beginner lessons. Students will become proficient in reading notes and rhythms.
• Simple Pieces: To foster a love for music, we introduce students to simple and enjoyable pieces that they can play with confidence.

Advanced Piano Lessons

For students who are looking to take their piano skills to the next level, our advanced lessons offer in-depth instruction and challenging repertoire.

• Complex Pieces: Students will tackle more advanced pieces, expanding their repertoire and enhancing their musical expression.
• Advanced Theory: Our lessons cover advanced music theory concepts, ensuring that students have a deep understanding of the music they play.
• Performance Preparation: We provide comprehensive performance preparation, helping students to develop their stage presence and confidence.
• Personalized Instruction: Each lesson is tailored to the individual student, ensuring that they receive the support needed to achieve their goals.
